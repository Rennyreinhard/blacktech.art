OVERVIEW AND HIGHLIGHTS

Founded in 2023,Dope Art Gallery has the vision of being the herculean art museum in Africa.
The museum collects, preserves, studies, and presents art in all media, from all
parts of the globe via the internet and one on one discussion.As at now Dope Art
Gallery is still a new brand under development to the later future hitherto most of the activities
would be carried out via the website using the internet.Keep in touch with us as we look forward to having a great time with you.
